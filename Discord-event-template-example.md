# 🏆 Shadow Ops Invitational

## 📋 Objective
Eliminate the opposing team's flag carrier before they reach the extraction point. First team to successfully extract **3 flags** takes the match.

## 👥 Teams
- **Team Alpha** — 5 players
- **Team Bravo** — 5 players

> Teams are assigned randomly at the start of each event. No swapping after the first round.

## 🎒 Loadouts
Each player selects **one** preset loadout before the match begins. Loadouts are locked for the duration of the event.

- `Scout` — Light armour, SMG, smoke grenades
- `Assault` — Medium armour, AR, frag grenades
- `Heavy` — Heavy armour, LMG, no grenades

## 🔄 Rounds
The event consists of **5 rounds**, each lasting a maximum of **10 minutes**.

- Rounds end early if a flag is successfully extracted
- Teams switch sides after every round
- No respawns — eliminate all enemies or extract to win the round

## 🥇 Event Win Condition
The team with the **most round wins** at the end of 5 rounds is declared the event winner.

- In the event of a tie, a **sudden death** tiebreaker round is played
- Tiebreaker uses randomised loadouts

## ➕ Additional Points
- +1 point for each enemy eliminated during extraction
- +2 bonus points awarded to the **MVP** of each round (voted by participants)
- Unsportsmanlike conduct results in point deductions at admin discretion
